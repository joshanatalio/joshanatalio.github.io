Jackit

Jackit is a iOS application that challeneges players from around the world in an intense shaking duel. It users the iPhone's gyroscope and accelerometer to create a quick and exciting gameplay experience for players. Player scores are calculated by how fast they can shake their phone in a ten second period. You can play solo in practice mode, or challenge another random player from all over the world! Become an all-star player and you'll be able to unlock some of the in-game achievement badges. Jackit also provides statistics for how each player performs, win-loss ratio, high score, etc.

Coded Primarily in Objective-C, Ruby on Rails.

Technologies Used: Xcode, Parse, REST API, Photoshop.

Dine

Dine is an iOS application built to enhance your dining experience. This companion can make a visit to your favorite restaurant faster, easier, and more enjoyable.

With Dine, you can:
  • Add your name to the restaurant's waiting list, and be notificed when your table is ready.
  • View beautiful pictures of each individual menu item, along with pricing, nutritional, and detailed dish information for each item.
  • Spontaneously build a meal using Dine's built in meal randomizer that allows you to set restrictions on a meal's price, calorie range, or other dietary preferences such as vegetarian or gluten-free entrees.
  • Pay the restaurant directly for your meal using Venmo (C), connected via Facebooks's AppLinks.

Dine was voted "Best Application" by a class of 200 CSE students.

Technologies Used: Xcode, Parse, AppLinks, iBeacon, Photoshop.
